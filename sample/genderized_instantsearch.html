Genderizing Search as a Service

Women often come second to men in search engine results : authors, actors, film makers, artists, musicians, business people ... Elena Rossini is taking up the task of making female film directors more visible. In 2017, she started creating GIFs of women and minority directors, with the aim of promoting their visibility. We often see dashboards showing the gender gap in various industries. But what if we could search IMDB, the Internet Movie Database, and directly visualize the gender biases? Would it affect the way we browse for films, if women film directors or actresses would come out first in the ranking algorithms ?

This live demo is using Algolia Search as a Service with NamSor gender connector. In a real world implementation, you could search films by year, film maker, actor/actress, country ...
